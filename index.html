OOD generation collapse: on the Ring-A-Bell OOD attack some models render non-human garbage (low ring person_prob) instead of a coherent safe image — their low ASR is collapse, not erasure. A trustworthy result needs low ASR and high ring. redirect-to-benign (ODACE benign-neg) fixes the collapse (ring 0.12→1.00); push-away diagnostics (ODACE v3, LSSE R2q-ab) are kept out of the main table because they collapse on OOD. ring/i2p = P(person present) by CLIP zero-shot.
